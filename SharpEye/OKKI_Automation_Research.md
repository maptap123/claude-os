## 2. EDM (Email Direct Marketing) Features

### Core Capabilities
- **Bulk sending with individual display (群發單顯):** Each recipient sees only their own name -- no visible CC list. Critical for cold outreach professionalism.
- **Multi-subject randomization:** AI generates up to 10 subject line variants for each campaign. Randomizing subjects across recipients significantly improves deliverability by avoiding spam filter pattern detection.
- **AI-randomized email body text:** Beyond subject lines, OKKI can randomize email body content to make each send unique, further evading spam filters.
- **Local time zone sending:** Emails are delivered based on the recipient's local time zone, not the sender's. This dramatically improves open rates.
- **Scheduled/delayed sending:** Campaigns can be scheduled in advance.

### Automated Drip Campaigns & Multi-Stage Sequences
- OKKI supports **multi-round automated touch sequences** across email, EDM, and WhatsApp.
- **Behavior-triggered automation:** Example workflow documented by OKKI:
  1. Send first email
  2. If unread after 7 days, automatically resend
  3. If opened, trigger 1:1 personalized follow-up
- **Nine documented follow-up progression strategies** are built into the platform's best practices, from routine feedback requests through to persistent six-month follow-up sequences.

### Template Creation
- Industry-specific templates available (product introduction, customer development, holiday greetings, general outreach).
- **AI Writing (AI寫信)** requires only 3 inputs: company name, product name, and key product highlights. Generates natural-sounding English emails in seconds.
- Templates support multilingual output (at least 9 languages documented in case studies).
- **Email health scoring:** Before sending, OKKI scores your email (1-10 scale). If the score is below 6, it recommends revising before sending -- catching spam triggers before they damage your reputation.

### Tracking & Analytics
- Real-time dashboard tracks: delivery rate, open rate, click rate, reply rate, bounce rate, and unsubscribe rate.
- "View failure reasons" for individual sends that didn't deliver.

## 5. Lead Discovery to Outreach Pipeline

### How OKKI Goes from Zero to Sent Email

**Path A: OKKI AiReach (Fully Automated)**
1. You input your product keywords and company profile (one-time setup).
2. AiReach generates an **industry supply chain graph** (行業圖譜), mapping upstream suppliers, downstream buyers, distributors, OEMs, and unexpected adjacent markets.
3. AiReach autonomously identifies prospects, performs AI background checks across 15+ data sources, and scores them for fit.
4. AiReach automatically drafts and sends personalized multilingual cold emails.
5. AiReach handles multi-round follow-up sequences autonomously.
6. Only prospects who reply with genuine interest are handed to you, with full interaction history.

**Path B: OKKI CRM (Manual/Semi-Automated Discovery)**
1. **Trade Data:** Access 1.8 billion+ customs and trade records.
2. **Google Maps:** Regional and nearby business search with company type filtering.
3. **B2B Inquiries:** Automatically pulls from overseas B2B platforms (limited to ~5 unlocks/day).
4. **Exhibition Data:** Historical exhibitor databases across industries.
5. **Social Media Mining:** Facebook/Instagram contact scraping via URLs.
6. **Search Engine Mining:** WhatsApp group discovery via public search.
7. From any discovery source, one click pushes the contact into an EDM campaign or one-to-one email sequence.

### Automation Level
- **AiReach is described as "L3 conditional autonomous driving"** -- it does the work autonomously but you can override or adjust at any time.
- The CRM side is more manual but still has automated workflows, scoring, and campaign triggers.
- The AiReach agent has **memory** -- it gets better at understanding your business the longer you use it.

### Proven Pipeline Results
- One company found **300+ potential buyers in 2 weeks** using AiReach.
- A manufacturer fed **5,000 dormant leads** into AiReach and achieved a **30%+ open rate** on cold re-engagement emails, discovering entirely new customer segments they hadn't considered.
- Average OKKI merchant increased active prospects by **35%** and conversion opportunities by **43.48%** in 2025.

## 6. Spam Prevention & Deliverability

### OKKI's Approach

**Infrastructure-Level**
- Emails are sent through **third-party delivery platforms** (not your own SMTP), which means sender reputation management is largely handled by OKKI/Alibaba's infrastructure.
- This is the same ecosystem that powers Alibaba.com's own email -- the sending IPs are established and trusted.

**Content-Level**
- **AI-randomized subjects and body text:** Makes every email unique, defeating pattern-based spam detection.
- **Email health scoring (1-10):** Pre-flight check before sending. If score <6, the platform recommends revising.
- **Test send feature:** Send to yourself or a colleague before campaign launch.
- **Contact verification (180-day badge):** Email addresses verified within 180 days are marked with a verification badge.

**Compliance Features**
- **Auto-suppression lists:** Bounces, unsubscribes, invalid addresses, and complaints are automatically suppressed.
- **Compliance with:** US CAN-SPAM, EU GDPR/ePrivacy, UK PECR, Canada CASL, plus Korea, Japan, Hong Kong, and Taiwan regulations.
- **Opt-out management:** Built-in unsubscribe handling with legal compliance for each jurisdiction.

**WhatsApp-Specific**
- Interval controls and per-account upper limits for bulk WhatsApp messaging.
- Multi-number auto-distribution to prevent any single number from being flagged.

### Domain Warming
- **Not explicitly documented.** OKKI's use of shared third-party sending infrastructure means traditional domain warming (gradually ramping your own domain's sending volume) is less critical than if you were sending from your own SMTP server. However, for best results:
  - Start with 50-100 emails/day in week 1.
  - Increase by 50-100/day each subsequent week.
  - Target 500-1,000/day by the end of month 3.

### What's NOT Handled
- You still need to bring (or purchase through Alibaba) a **sending domain** with proper SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records configured.
- The quality of your prospect list still matters -- sending to invalid or purchased email lists will hurt deliverability regardless of platform.

## 8. Recommendations for August

### Recommended Setup

**Phase 1: Foundation (Week 1-2)**
1. Sign up for the OKKI AiReach free 2-month trial (2,000 leads/month included).
2. Start with a small OKKI CRM tier (1-2 users). Request a quote from Alibaba sales.
3. Configure your sending domain with SPF, DKIM, DMARC.
4. Upload Sharp-Eye's product catalog: CNC machining, milling, turning, 5-axis capabilities, materials (aluminum, stainless steel, titanium, etc.), tolerances, certifications, industries served.
5. Define target markets: Start with 3-5 countries (US, Germany, Japan, UK, Australia) and 5-10 industries (automotive, medical devices, aerospace, industrial equipment, robotics, semiconductor equipment, etc.).

**Phase 2: AI Prospecting Launch (Week 2-4)**
1. Activate AiReach with your product profile and let it generate the supply chain graph.
2. Let AiReach run its first round of automated prospecting and email outreach.
3. Meanwhile, use OKKI CRM's trade data and Google Maps discovery to manually build a prospect list of 200-500 companies in your priority industries.
4. Upload existing contacts and past inquiries into OKKI CRM for AI reactivation.

**Phase 3: EDM Campaigns (Week 3-8)**
1. Launch your first EDM campaign to the manually built list (start with 50-100/day, ramp up weekly).
2. Create 3-4 email templates: general CNC capabilities introduction, industry-specific (medical device machining, automotive parts), and a follow-up sequence.
3. Use AI-randomized subjects and body text for every campaign.
4. Set up automated behavior-triggered sequences: if no open in 7 days, resend; if opened but no reply, trigger a different follow-up after 5 days.

**Phase 4: Scale (Month 2-3)**
1. By month 3, aim for **500-1,000 cold emails/day** across AiReach automation + manual EDM campaigns.
2. Layer in WhatsApp outreach for high-priority prospects identified by AiReach.
3. Use OKKI's analytics to identify which industries and countries have the highest reply rates, then double down.

### Realistic Daily Volume August Can Achieve

| Timeframe | Daily Cold Emails | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Week 1-2 | 50-100 | Manual EDM (warming phase) |
| Week 3-4 | 200-300 | EDM + AiReach auto-sends |
| Month 2 | 400-600 | EDM scaled + AiReach active |
| Month 3+ | 500-1,000 | Full EDM capacity + AiReach running autonomously |

**Annual projection at 500-1,000/day:** 120,000-240,000 touchpoints per year.

This is achievable for a solo operator because:
- AiReach runs autonomously 24/7 -- August doesn't write each email.
- The multi-channel inbox consolidates all replies into one place.
- AI background checks eliminate hours of manual prospect research.
- Only genuinely interested replies reach August's attention.

### What NOT to Do
- Do not blast 1,000 emails on day one. Even with OKKI's infrastructure, ramp gradually.
- Do not skip email health scoring before each campaign.
- Do not send to purchased email lists -- OKKI's suppression system and spam filters will penalize you.
- Do not ignore WhatsApp and Facebook as supplementary channels. Multi-channel touchpoints significantly improve overall response rates.
