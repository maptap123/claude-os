## Pricing Standards

*JDC's current defaults — confirmed from 2026 sample estimates (May 2026 research).*

- **Markup on remodels:** 50% across the board (yields 33.33% margin). Applied uniformly to materials, labor, and subcontract lines in the cost-code system.
- **Markup on new builds:** Currently 20–25% (varies by job — Cameron 2024 at 20%, Wagner/Wood/Crossing 2026 at 25%, Masters at 20%). **Open question:** August wants this reviewed before next new-build estimate — do not default without confirming.
- **Margin formula:** `markup / (100 + markup)`. So 50% markup = 33.33% margin; 25% markup = 20% margin; 20% markup = 16.67% margin.
- **Tax:** All sample lines reviewed are non-taxable.
- **Plans/Permits (01.0000.010):** Sometimes priced (e.g., $500–$1,200) and sometimes zeroed in Total Cost. Confirm per-job whether to charge or absorb.
- **Contingency:** Not seen as a standard line item in 2026 sample estimates. Confirm with August whether to add one.
