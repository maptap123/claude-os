## Key Decisions

- **TWD → USD conversion**: Convert at ~31.5 TWD/USD for 2026 statements. Update this rate if the user provides a different one.
- **Transfers between accounts**: Categorized as `Transfers` and excluded from spending totals. This includes credit card payments (checking → CC) and PayPal funding moves.
- **Roth IRA activity**: Logged but treated as `Investments` (a Transfers-style bucket) — not counted in monthly spending.
- **Categorization defaults**: Use the taxonomy in Personal Spending Tracker.xlsx → Category Taxonomy tab. When uncertain, flag with `Needs Review` rather than guessing.
